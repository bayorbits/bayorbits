# Commercial License Summary (Non-Binding Overview)

> This file is a practical summary for developers.
> It is **not** the full legal agreement.

## Purpose

Bay Orbits offers a paid commercial license as an alternative to GPLv3 for organizations that need proprietary distribution terms.

## Rights typically granted under commercial terms

- Use in proprietary or closed-source products.
- Integration into internal or customer-facing proprietary systems.
- Distribution without GPLv3 copyleft source-disclosure obligations (as defined in the executed commercial agreement).

## What this does **not** include

- Transfer of copyright ownership.
- Automatic trademark rights.
- Permission to repackage and resell Bay Orbits itself as a competing standalone product, unless explicitly granted in writing.

## How to obtain a commercial license

Contact: **licensing@bayorbits.com**

Include in your message:
- organization name,
- intended use case,
- distribution model,
- expected deployment scope.

Bay Orbits will provide pricing and the full commercial agreement for review.

## Conflict rule

If there is any conflict between this summary and a signed commercial agreement, the signed agreement controls.
